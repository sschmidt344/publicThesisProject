2. Viruses constantly change through mutation.

3. Delta is a variant of the virus that causes COVID-19.
4. Delta spreads more easily than other variants
which could lead to more cases of COVID-19.
5. The best way to stop the spread of Delta is to get vaccinated against COVID-19.
6. FDA-authorized COVID-19 vaccines offer protection against Delta and other variants.
